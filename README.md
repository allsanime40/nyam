## 📖 About Nyam

Nyam is a cat-focused mobile application developed to provide cat lovers with reliable, easy-to-understand, and engaging information about cats. Whether you are a first-time cat owner, an experienced pet parent, or simply someone who loves cats, Nyam offers valuable resources to help you better understand and care for your feline companions.

The application combines educational content, practical care guides, and breed information in a simple and user-friendly interface, making cat knowledge accessible to everyone.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

The main objectives of Nyam are:

* Promote responsible cat ownership.
* Educate users about proper cat care and welfare.
* Provide accurate information about cat breeds and characteristics.
* Help users understand cat health, nutrition, and behavior.
* Encourage a stronger bond between cats and their owners.
* Create awareness about animal welfare and pet responsibility.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🐾 Cat Breed Explorer

Browse detailed information about various cat breeds, including:

* Origin and history
* Physical characteristics
* Temperament and personality
* Lifespan
* Grooming requirements
* Activity levels

### 🍽️ Feeding & Nutrition Guide

Learn about:

* Proper feeding schedules
* Recommended food types
* Nutritional requirements
* Foods that cats should avoid
* Hydration and healthy eating habits

### 🩺 Health & Wellness Information

Access useful health-related information such as:

* Common cat health issues
* Preventive care tips
* Vaccination awareness
* Basic hygiene practices
* Signs of illness to watch for

### 😺 Cat Behavior Guide

Understand your cat better through information about:

* Body language
* Communication signals
* Play behavior
* Social interactions
* Stress indicators

### 📚 Educational Resources

Explore articles and guides covering:

* Cat care fundamentals
* Indoor and outdoor safety
* New kitten preparation
* Senior cat care
* Responsible pet ownership

### 🔍 Search Functionality

Quickly find information using a simple search system designed for convenience and efficiency.

## 📱 Why Nyam?

Cats are among the most popular pets worldwide, yet many owners lack access to reliable and easy-to-understand information about their care. Nyam aims to bridge this gap by providing a centralized platform where users can learn about cat breeds, health, nutrition, and behavior in a convenient and engaging way.

By empowering users with knowledge, Nyam helps improve the quality of life for cats while fostering responsible pet ownership.
